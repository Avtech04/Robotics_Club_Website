ROBOMANIA

(AVISHKAR)

Step into the vibrant world of Avishkar, MNNIT's annual techno-management extravaganza, where innovation and brilliance collide! Happening in the spirited month of March, Avishkar is the ultimate playground for students to sharpen their technical and managerial skills, sculpt outstanding projects, weave valuable networks, and sculpt a stellar CV.

This dynamic festival is not just a celebration within the walls of MNNIT â€“ it's a nationwide showcase of talent. Every department and technical club at MNNIT hosts riveting competitions, drawing participants from across the nation. These contests are the battlegrounds where aspiring technocrats engage in fierce battles, embodying the spirit of "Ideate, Innovate and Create".

Apart from these competitions, many inspiring talks by eminent people in the industry and academia and other activities also happen during Avishkar, enlightening and preparing the participants for many challenges that lie ahead.

As a technical club, Robotics Club MNNIT organizes many challenging competitions during Avishkar under the name Robomania:

CozmoClench

"Immerse yourself in the heart-pounding spectacle of CozmoClench where participants wield custom-built RC remotes, guiding their robots through a dynamic course filled with challenges. Watch as they conquer obstacles with precision and skill in this thrilling showcase of creativity and robotics mastery."

HACK-QUEST

"Dive into the electrifying realm of HACK-QUEST, where innovation meets action! Unleash the force of your creativity and expertise in AI and ML as you navigate real-world challenges on this dynamic platform. Join the adventure, unravel intricacies, and let your inventive models shine in the captivating landscape of HACK-QUEST."

Track-Trek

"Step into the realm of Track-Trek, a creative challenge where participants design a bot using Arduino and sensors. Their mission? Guide the bot along a predetermined path from start to finish, discovering the shortest route in a race against time. Join us for this exciting fusion of creativity and technology!"

Navigate the Dark

"Embark on the enigmatic journey of Navigate the Dark! Craft a bot using ESP32 and its camera, then navigate through a maze relying solely on the camera feed. Encounter obstacles, solve the maze, and conquer this thrilling challenge that merges technology, strategy, and suspense."

Detect N Direct

"Get ready to unleash your robotic prowess at Detect N Direct, where participants detect circles and do precision aiming with a laser at progressively shrinking targets using OpenCV-Python and Arduino. Join us for an electrifying experience where innovation meets precision at Detect N Direct."
